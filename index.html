Hand Gestured Robotic Arm

A hand-gestured robotic arm is a type of robotic arm that can be controlled by the movements of a person's hand. This technology typically involves using sensors to detect the position and movement of the user's hand, and then translating those movements into corresponding movements of the robotic arm.
